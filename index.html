Virtualization

Organizations are moving their infrastructure either to virtual infrastructure or to cloud depending on their current and future needs. Virtualization helps reduce total cost of ownership of the IT services by optimizing server use, reducing energy costs, allowing deployment of workloads faster and providing more high availability and disaster recovery options. We provide following virtualization services:

  • Expansion of your existing virtual infrastructure
  • Migration of all the services from physical servers to virtual servers
  • Installation of new virtual servers
